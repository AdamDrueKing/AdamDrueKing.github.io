Hi, again.

I've always been a curious one. That curiosity has been rooted in how things are made, and what makes them work. This has lead me to a life as a "maker," in many different mediums. Spending a large part of my life designing, and making, clothes as been very fulfilling and pride-filled. However, my curiosity has grown in wanting to see things I build do *more*. While working full-time I started developing an interest in computer coding. I've worked as a Design Director for the past few years, and decided to persue my new passion as a developer. This is when I became a student/apprentice at WeCanCodeIt. I worked with .NET and developed my coding skills in C# as well as HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, ASP.NET MVC5, mySQL and Unity.
